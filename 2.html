Frequently Asked Questions

Anyone who meets the eligibility criteria defined by Leveled can invest. This typically includes being of legal age and meeting specific financial or accreditation requirements.

The minimum investment amount varies depending on the specific SMB opportunity but generally starts at $1,000.

Returns vary by investment but can include both passive income from dividends and potential long-term capital appreciation.

Leveled earns revenue through a combination of management fees, platform fees, and a share of the profits on certain investment opportunities.

Investment horizons depend on the SMB and can range from a few months to several years, as outlined in the investment details.

Liquidity options are limited and depend on the specific terms of the SMB investment. Some investments may offer buy-back options or secondary market sales.

Leveled conducts thorough due diligence, evaluating SMBs based on financial performance, market potential, and operational stability.
